Warranty Conditions

Comprehensive warranty coverage with industry-leading terms and conditions

PV Module Warranty - 30 YEARS

  • • 12 years product warranty
  • • 30 years performance warranty (90% up to 10 years, 80% up to 25 years)
  • • Product certification to IEC 61215 (ed. 2) protection class II / IEC 617
  • • 90% performance guaranteed up to 10 years
  • • 80% performance guaranteed up to 25 years

Warranty of Balance Systems

  • • Deye/Ksolar Inverter: 10 years warranty from the date of supplying in accordance with warranty conditions by the inverter manufacturer
  • • Electrical installation works: 5 years warranty from the date of commissioning
  • • Interconnection of strings, connection of string combiner boxes, and AC/DC Cables: 5 years warranty

Repair & Replacement Process

Parts and components, which are repaired or replaced during such period, are warranted for the original warranty period only and will not carry any extended warranty.

Service Procedure: If the equipment is not repairable at the site, the same has to be sent to the Service center on a freight prepaid basis after obtaining consent from Murickens Trading Company in writing and Murickens Trading Company shall make good the defect and return back to the Customer on freight to pay basis.

The customer shall provide all the necessary site information and structural particulars for design of the panel structural and load bearing assessment of the location.

Warranty Exclusions

This warranty shall not apply to defects resulting from:

  • • Willful damage or negligence, normal wear and tear
  • • Natural calamities
  • • Installation and/or maintenance by Customer or a third party
  • • Misuse or abuse of Equipment
  • • Modifications or alterations made by Customer or a third party without consent from Murickens Trading Company
